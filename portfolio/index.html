About Me

Hello! My name is Breland Norwood, and I am a passionate Web Development and Design major at the University of Arkansas at Little Rock. Ever since I discovered the intricate world of website creation, I have been captivated by the possibilities of combining creativity, technical expertise, and user experience to create stunning digital masterpieces. Throughout my academic journey, I have honed my skills in various programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as gained expertise in popular web frameworks and libraries like Bootstrap. In addition to my technical knowledge, I have a keen eye for design, which allows me to create visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces that effectively communicate the intended message. When I am not busy studying or coding, I enjoy volunteering my web development and design expertise to local non-profit organizations, helping them establish a strong online presence. This has given me the opportunity to work on real-world projects, enhance my portfolio, and contribute positively to my community. As I continue to grow and learn in this ever-evolving field, I am eager to take on challenging projects and internships that will push me to further develop my skills and knowledge. Ultimately, my goal is to become a leading web developer and designer, creating innovative and impactful digital solutions that improve the lives of others. Thank you for visiting my page, and feel free to browse through my portfolio and reach out if you'd like to discuss potential collaborations or job opportunities. Let's create something amazing together!
